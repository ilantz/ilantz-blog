Welcome to my blog! I'm Ilan Lanz, a technology enthusiast, systems architect, and cybersecurity professional with over two decades of experience. 

### About This Blog
I started publishing my posts on this blog in 2008. My goal was to share my experience and knowledge with the community. I typically blog about topics and issues that I've found hard to find answers to, had to figure out myself through trial and error, and believed that others might benefit from my findings.
