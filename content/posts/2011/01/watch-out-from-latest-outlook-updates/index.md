There has been a growing concern about the issues from latest Outlook 2007 / 2010 updates:
{{< /lead >}}

[http://blogs.office.com/b/microsoft-outlook/archive/2010/12/17/issues-with-the-recent-update-for-outlook-2007.aspx](http://blogs.office.com/b/microsoft-outlook/archive/2010/12/17/issues-with-the-recent-update-for-outlook-2007.aspx)

Microsoft has released the updated [http://support.microsoft.com/kb/2412171](http://support.microsoft.com/kb/2412171) KB, but seems not all trouble were solved, mainly the AutoDiscover issue, since [http://support.microsoft.com/kb/2479671](http://support.microsoft.com/kb/2479671) was released followed by the "original" 2412171KB numerous issues have been identified with outlook features.

I'd advice to stay away from these updates until all is clear.
