To follow my previous blog "[Watch out from latest outlook updates !](http://ilantz.com/2011/01/26/watch-out-from-latest-outlook-updates/)"

the Exchange team has announced that the [Office 2007 Cumulative Update for February 2011](http://msexchangeteam.com/archive/2011/02/28/458375.aspx) is now available.

The update adds Personal Archives support in Outlook 2007. For more details about the hotfix, see KBA [2475891: Description of the Office Outlook 2007 hotfix package (Outlook-x-none.msp): February 22, 2011.](http://support.microsoft.com/kb/2475891) and also fixes quite a lot of issues that were presented from the original outlook December hotfix, Autodiscover issues, POP3 Authentication issues and few more..

As always ! test your hotfix !
